Three Poems

Wheeler Light

October 1, 2018

Harbor Town

There is a town filled with red houses filled with bandages.
Each house looks like a small seaside hospital. Someone is
running around using a stretcher as a shield. Patients are
dying in every red house. Before any body can be buried it
disappears. I am trying to make too much sense of this.

 
                                  I am beaten to death with a fish.
