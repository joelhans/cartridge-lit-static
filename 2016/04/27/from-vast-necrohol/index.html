Caolan Madden is a PhD candidate in English literature at Rutgers University. She has an MFA from Johns Hopkins, which is where she first encountered Final Fantasy XII's Necrohol of Nabudis. Her writing has appeared in Triple Canopy, Bone Bouquet, Iron Horse Literary Review, The Great Gatsby Anthology, and WEIRD SISTER, where she is a contributing editor. She lives in Brooklyn with her husband and daughter.
