Jordan Pailthorpe is a third year MFA candidate in poetry and adjunct instructor at Emerson College. His work attempts to blur the line between poetics and game culture through writing inside an electronic environment and bringing the form of the electronic environment onto the page. He is co-founder of a new multimodal student journal on games and play called Level257 and works at the Emerson Engagement Lab as a game designer and project coordinator.
