]]> Harbor Town

There is a town filled with red houses filled with bandages.
Each house looks like a small seaside hospital. Someone is
running around using a stretcher as a shield. Patients are
dying in every red house. Before any body can be buried it
disappears. I am trying to make too much sense of this.

 
                                  I am beaten to death with a fish.

Bug Update

I can’t get out of the car
and the car can’t move.

We are both in a tree
and we can’t get out.

There is a cottage
             in the distance.

A body dangling out a window
        disappears.

The car explodes.

Ring Progress

You decide to settle
in a small town

but your car gets stolen
and your house bombed.

It is far away but
a blue hole
gets smaller.
